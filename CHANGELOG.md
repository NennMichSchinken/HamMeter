# Changelog

## 1.0.3
Reworked the job tags with a glass look. The border and text now follow each job's colour so they stay readable.
Fixed the bar gradient not reaching the rounded left/right ends.

## 1.0.2
Internal cleanup of the plugin's naming. As part of this, HamMeter's settings are reset to their defaults once — you'll need to reapply any custom colours or layout changes after updating.

## 1.0.1
Unified the look of the dialogs and dropdowns across the meter and the settings window, with consistent rounded corners and colours.

## 1.0.0
First public release.
